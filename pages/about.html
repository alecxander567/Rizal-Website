✦ A Message From the Team

Third Year BSIT

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

This website is our final project for our Life and Works of Rizal subject — and it became something we genuinely cared about making well. What started as a course requirement turned into our own quiet tribute to a man who believed that knowledge, honestly presented, is the most powerful thing a person can offer their country.

We are students of technology, but Rizal taught us that tools only matter when they are put in service of something true. We hope this project honors his story with the care it deserves.
